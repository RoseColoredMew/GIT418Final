Cleaning & Weekly Maintenance

Keep your pool sparkling clean with our professional maintenance services. We offer weekly and one-time visits, chemical balancing, and restorative rinses to keep your pool swim-ready.

  • Weekly service and one-time visits
  • Chemical testing & balancing (water testing)
  • Filter cleaning and quarterly maintenance
  • Acid / chlorine rinses and stain removal
  • Green pool recovery and algae treatment
  • Spa & pool drain service

Repairs & Equipment

From minor fixes to major equipment upgrades, our experienced technicians can handle all your pool repair needs quickly and efficiently.

  • Equipment installation, upgrades & replacement
  • Inspections, diagnostics, and leak detection
  • Light installation and repair
  • Heater repair and tuning
  • Automation, control setup & equipment troubleshooting
